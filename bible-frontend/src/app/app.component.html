Welcome to Daily Bible Reading

I created this app to share what I’ve learned about the importance of daily devotion—spending time with God through His Word.

It's a simple yet powerful practice: read one chapter from Proverbs, one from the Old Testament, and one from the New Testament each day.

I pray this app guides and encourages you on your own journey. May God bless your time in His Word!
